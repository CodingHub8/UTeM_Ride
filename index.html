Features Designed for UTeM

A safe, efficient, and cost-effective transportation ecosystem for campuses.

Verified UTeM Circle Only

Strictly secure authentication utilizing UTeM domain emails (`@student.utem.edu.my` or `@utem.edu.my`) paired with Matric Card OCR verification to shield the campus community.

Real-time Interactive Maps

Passenger location pinning, live coordinates routing, and destination matching mapping between Kampus Induk, Kampus Teknologi, and nearby student hubs.

Secure E-Wallet Payments

Cashless convenience. Supports Cash, Secure Visa/Mastercard payments, and FPX Online Banking, crediting drivers directly upon ride completion.

RM 1.00 Payout Minimums

Drivers can withdraw earnings to saved bank details at any time with a low minimum limit of only RM 1.00, keeping funds accessible when needed.

10% Clear Flat Commission

No hidden charges. Rides completions credit drivers automatically, deducting a flat 10% app platform fee transparently logged under e-wallet ledger details.

Gender Matching Security

Safety-focused options allowing female passengers to book exclusively with female drivers and vice-versa, offering ultimate peace of mind during transit.

How It Works

Simplified steps for getting your ride or sharing your car.

For Passengers

  • 1

    Request a Ride

    Pin your pickup point on the map, select destination (e.g. FTMK, Kampus Induk), choose payment method (FPX, Card, or Cash).

  • 2

    Match with Driver

    Get matched with a verified student driver traveling along the same route. Live tracking updates as the driver approaches.

  • 3

    Complete & Paid

    Reach your destination safely. E-wallet balances are automatically adjusted, or cash is paid directly to the driver.

For Drivers

  • 1

    Upload Driving Documents

    Register using UTeM matric card, add vehicle details, and upload road tax documents. Approvals are processed within 1-7 business days.

  • 2

    Accept Commuting Rides

    Switch to Driver Mode, view incoming student ride requests, and accept routes matching your daily academic schedule.

  • 3

    Earn & Payout

    Keep 90% of the fare. Fares are credited to your in-app e-wallet. Request payout to your saved bank account for amounts as low as RM 1.00.
